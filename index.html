Pera Helping Hands(PHH) is a charity organisation
run by University of Peradeniya UK alumni.
Our mission is to help our community
by bringing together people with various
skills and knowledge and using their expertise to find
sustainable solutions to various socio-economic problems in our society.

Our Goal

We are a member-driven, transparent organization focused on collaboration, empowering individuals to contribute through knowledge, not money. Members propose projects, enhancing self-improvement and learning. Through interdisciplinary project reviews, we link knowledge creators with consumers, addressing complex social issues with education, job creation, and knowledge transfer.

We Aim to Think and Act Differently to Efficiently bring Lasting Change
